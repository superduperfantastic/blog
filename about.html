Hi, I’m Tinna (one 'n' is silent), but I sign my name as “Suki” all over the interwebs, including Twitter. By day, I am a number cruncher and master of Excel working for a social enterprise based in San Francisco. The rest of the time, I’m dreaming about my next travel destination or small house project.

I’m a native San Franciscan living with the boyfriend and our two animals in what I consider to be “home base,” despite its daily lessons on the joys of homeownership.

I spend most weekends and any time off: volunteering, learning, sipping tasty beverages, getting outdoors, eating around the world and documenting travels and other adventures.

Once upon a time, I had major jaw surgery. That journey’s documented on this blog as well!
